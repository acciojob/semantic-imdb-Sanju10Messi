Iron Man is a Marvel superhero alter-ego of billionaire genius Tony Stark, who creates a high-tech armored suit to escape captivity and protect the world, famously initiating the Marvel Cinematic Universe in 2008. Known for his wit and bravery, Stark uses advanced technology like the Arc Reactor to power his suits.
